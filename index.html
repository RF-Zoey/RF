About Rightful Family

Rightful Family is a gaming community founded on October 28, 2024, led by Zoey. We’re here to offer a welcoming space where adult gamers can enjoy connecting and competing in their favorite games without the hassle of a younger crowd.

What Games We Play

Our main games include Clash of Clans, Apex Legends, Rainbow Six Siege, Minecraft, and Pokémon GO. We offer both casual and competitive experiences for all these games, so no matter what you’re into, there’s a place for you here.

What Our Goals Are

Our goal is to keep things fun and active, so you can hop on whenever, find people to game with, and start building some real connections. We’re all about making friendships that go beyond the game—so join us, and let’s make Rightful Family your go-to gaming crew!
